About EZ Request

Streamlining your organizational workflow, one request at a time.

What is EZ Request?

EZ Request is a comprehensive management platform designed to simplify the complex processes of service requests, approvals, and employee management within organizations. It serves as a centralized hub where employees can submit various requests—from leave applications to reimbursement claims—and track their progress in real-time.

Why use it?

Efficiency

Eliminate paper trails and email chains. Submit and approve requests with just a few clicks.

Transparency

Real-time status updates ensure everyone knows exactly where a request stands in the pipeline.

Accountability

Clear approval hierarchies (Manager & Director) ensure proper oversight for every action.

Accessibility

Access your dashboard from anywhere, anytime. Designed for the modern workforce.
